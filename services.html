Services

I am pleased to be collaborating with Parno Family Chiropractic to offer a more holistic approach to breastfeeding, pediatric and postpartum health. My office is located off 494 and Valley Creek Road in Woodbury, where you you will find ample free parking and a space thoughtfully designed for your privacy and comfort. Flexible meeting and scheduling options are available. I am currently following recommended COVID-19 guidelines.

Initial in-home visits are available for infants less than 14 days old. In-home visits are also available under special circumstances. All in-home visits are schedule permitting. Please contact me to request an in-home visit. An additional travel fee of $150 is applied to all in-home visit except for initial newborn visits within a 15 mile radius of my office.

All initial visits include:

  • A feeding plan designed for you and your family
  • Assessment of baby’s weight
  • Unlimited support via text or email for two weeks following the home visit
  • Communication with your healthcare provider as appropriate

Follow-up visits and back-to-work consults

In-office (1 hr) $190
In-home (2 hrs) $265
Virtual (45 min) $140
Evening, weekend or holiday appts. +$60

Follow-up visits and back-to-work consults are scheduled as needed following an initial visit. They typically include:

  • A detailed check-in on how things are going
  • Continued monitoring of your baby’s weight
  • Troubleshooting if necessary
  • Future planning (i.e. return to work, scheduling, etc.)

Prenatal visits

Virtual (20 min) Free

Complimentary virtual prenatal visits include a brief introductory video call to answer any questions/concerns you may have and to discuss working together to meet your feeding goals. They provide an opportunity to:

  • Get to know each other
  • Answer your questions/concerns regarding feeding
  • Help develop a feeding “game plan”
  • Evaluate how to set up your home to make feeding easier
  • Assist in choosing and learning how to use your breast pump
  • …and more!

Billing info

At the end of every visit, I will provide you with a medical bill that you can submit to your insurance company. Reimbursement is not guaranteed. I accept all major credit cards and HSA/FSA cards via Square. Gift cards are also available for purchase.

As per the State of MinnesotaCare Tax (Minnesota Statutes. Sec. 295.50), a 1.8% tax is applied to all lactation visits at the time of service.

Due to the inconvenience cancellations cause, a $150 fee will be applied to your card if you cancel or reschedule your appointment with less than 48 hours notice. An appointment "no-show" will result in a charge to your card on file for the full cost of the visit.

Insurance

Insurance coverage is available through The Lactation Network for those who qualify. TLN guarantees 100% coverage for up to six visits. Click here to see if you are eligible.

Appointments tailored for you and your baby may include:

  • Weighted feeds to assess how much milk your baby is transferring at the breast
  • Assistance with positioning, latching, manual expression, and more
  • Guidance and hands-on instruction on the proper use of your breast pump
  • Pump flange fitting
  • Using breastfeeding tools to optimize your breastfeeding experience (nipple shield, nipple everter, etc)
  • Feeding more than one baby (tandem feeding, feeding schedule for multiples, triple feeding, etc.)
  • Troubleshooting feeding problems specific to parent and baby dyad
  • Prescription treatment for common breastfeeding problems without a visit to your healthcare provider
  • Establishing and supporting chest-feeding
  • Evidence-based guidance on how to manage your milk supply (boosting supply, decreasing supply, weaning, storing milk, etc.)
  • Supporting your transition back to work as a breastfeeding parent and developing a plan as needed
  • Instruction on how best to bottle feed a breastfed baby
  • Hands on instruction on supplementation (cup/spoon feeding, syringe feeding, finger feeding, SNS usage, paced bottle feeding, etc.)
  • Referral to and communication with your healthcare provider when additional medical care is required
